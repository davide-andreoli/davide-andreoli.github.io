Hi, I’m Davide!

My background is in chemistry and semiconductor process engineering, but over the years I found myself increasingly fascinated by data, software, and AI. What started as using data to better understand industrial processes eventually became a full transition into data engineering.

Today I build data platforms, analytics solutions, and AI-powered tools, with a particular interest in the intersection between industrial knowledge and modern data technologies.

I created this website to share projects, experiments, and blog posts about data engineering, AI engineering, and data applications in the process industry, and all the things I learn along the way.

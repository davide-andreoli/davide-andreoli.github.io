My name is Davide, nice to meet you! 

I'm a chemist first, a data engineer second, and a tech enthusiast overall.
I studied chemistry at the university of Milan, and I like to think that I see the world through a chemical lens, even when I'm not working with molecules.
I started my career as a process engineer in the semiconductor industry, where I had the chance of working on process development and optimization, which made me realize how much I enjoy working with data to solve problems and gain insights. This eventually led me to transition into data engineering, where I built a data platform, analytics solutions, and AI-powered tools.
